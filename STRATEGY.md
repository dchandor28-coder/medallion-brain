# ⚔️ STRATEGY — The Medallion Court Playbook

## Mission Restatement

We are not here to grind a slow profit. We are here to **execute 6 consecutive winning bets** using a full-purse martingale, turning X into 64X. This requires:

1. **Extreme selectivity** — We may bet 2-4 times per week, sometimes less
2. **High per-bet win probability** — 65%+ true probability on every bet
3. **Contrarian positioning** — We bet against the crowd when the math supports it
4. **Information superiority** — We know more context than the casual bettor
5. **Emotional discipline** — No tilt, no FOMO, no forcing action

## Bet Types in Priority Order

### 1. SPREAD (Primary — ~80% of bets)
- Clearest edge identification
- Most liquid market
- Best historical data for validation
- We prefer taking points (underdogs) but will lay points if the edge is clear

### 2. TOTAL (Secondary — ~15% of bets)
- Use when the spread edge is unclear but pace/style matchup gives a strong O/U read
- Unders tend to have slightly better contrarian value (public loves overs)
- Must have quant support — "feel" is not enough for totals

### 3. MONEYLINE (Rare — ~5% of bets)
- Only when we believe an underdog will WIN OUTRIGHT (not just cover)
- The + moneyline provides better payout for the same risk
- Use when underdog ML gives us better expected value than the spread
- NEVER take favorite moneyline (laying juice for the same effective bet)

  - March Madness first round 10-12 seeds vs chalk

## The Anti-Patterns — What We NEVER Do

```
❌ NEVER bet a game just because we haven't bet in 3+ days
❌ NEVER bet multiple games in one day (one game, one focus)
❌ NEVER split our purse across sides or games
❌ NEVER bet an early game AND a late game as "backup"
❌ NEVER override the conviction threshold (9/14) for any reason
❌ NEVER bet a game where a key player is truly questionable at T-30min
❌ NEVER increase bet size beyond our purse (no borrowing, no margin)
❌ NEVER bet live/in-game (we are a pregame system)
❌ NEVER change our pick after placing the bet (no hedging, no cashing out)
❌ NEVER bet based on a single agent's recommendation (requires system agreement)
```

## Post-Bet Protocol

### After a WIN
```
1. Update streak tracker (N+1 / 6)
2. Calculate new purse (double previous)
3. Log game, bet, result, and all agent scores
4. Begin scanning for next opportunity
5. DO NOT rush the next bet. The streak growing does not change our standards.
6. If anything, be MORE selective as the streak grows (more to lose)
```

### After a LOSS
```
1. Reset streak to 0/6
2. Reset purse to starting amount
3. Log game, bet, result, and all agent scores
4. PERFORM POSTMORTEM:
   - Which agent(s) were wrong?
   - Was the loss due to bad process or bad luck?
   - If bad process: What signal did we miss? Adjust.
   - If bad luck: The edge was real, the outcome was variance. Continue.
5. Wait at least 24 hours before placing the next bet (cool-down period)
6. Begin new attempt
```
